
CANADA-BRAZIL TRADE REPORT: 2024-2025
================================================================================

OVERVIEW:
Trade between Canada and Brazil reached $14,470,332,741 in 2025, 
representing a +16.0% change from 2024 ($12,476,738,128).

KEY DRIVERS:
• Primary growth engine: Ontario (68.8% of total growth)
• Leading product category: Cocoa and Cocoa Preparations (+212.2%)
• Market concentration: Top 10 provinces drive 80% of growth

OUTLOOK:
• 6-month forecast: +2.2% growth expected
• Projected 6-month total: $7,641,817,188
• Confidence level: HIGH (R²=0.960, MAPE=2.5%)

STRATEGIC IMPERATIVE:
Focus resources on Ontario and Cocoa and Cocoa Preparations to maximize impact, 
as these two areas combined represent the majority of growth opportunity.
