
Canada–Brazil bilateral trade reached $14,470,332,741 in 2025, representing a +16.0% increase from 2024. 
This growth has been driven primarily by Ontario and Cocoa and Cocoa Preparations, which together account for the majority of the expansion.

• Trade growth: +16.0% (2024–2025)
• Primary growth engine: Ontario (68.8% of total growth)
• Leading product category: Cocoa and Cocoa Preparations (+212.2%)
• Market concentration: Top 3 provinces drive 80% of growth

OUTLOOK:
